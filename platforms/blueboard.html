NGX Blueboard LPC1768-H¶

To build for the Blueboard, compile with the flag PLATFORM=BLUEBOARD.

UART¶

Identical to the Ford prototype.

Debug Logging¶

On the Blueboard LPC1768H, logging will be on UART0 (Pin P0.3 - Rx, Pin P0.2 - Tx) at 115200 baud.

LED Lights¶

Currently LEDs are not supported on the Blueboard
